Delicious Fish Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g fish (any type)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili, coriander, cumin)
  • Salt to taste

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Marinate the fish with turmeric and salt.
  2. Heat oil and saut√© onions till golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add marinated fish and cook gently.
  5. Pour water and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
